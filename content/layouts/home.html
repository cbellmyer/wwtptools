[ About ]

Built for
operators,
not vendors

Every calculator here is one I use on shift. Every reference table is something I've had to look up enough times that I finally put it somewhere permanent.

A lot of "technical resources" in this industry are vendor documentation dressed as neutral information — everything from equipment manuals to "educational guides" is ultimately trying to sell you chemicals, equipment, or consulting. This site exists outside that model. No vendor relationships, no affiliate links, no content that exists to sell you a product.

Written by an operator, for operators. Free, open source, and built to work on a phone in the middle of a process upset.
