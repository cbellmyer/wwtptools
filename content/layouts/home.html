[ About ]

Built from the floor up

These tools exist because I needed them and couldn't find them in one place.

I hold a Maryland Class 5A Wastewater License — the highest operator grade in the state — earned two years ahead of the standard track. Before that: environmental field work, GIS, emergency response mapping during Hurricane Maria, and a decade of learning that the right information at the right time is everything.

The calculators here are the same ones I use on shift. The reference tables are things I've had to look up enough times that I finally put them somewhere permanent. The guides are written the way I wish someone had explained things to me earlier in my career.

Everything is free, open source, and built to work on a phone in the middle of a process upset.
